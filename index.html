تَقَبَّلَ اللَّهُ مِنَّا وَمِنكُمْ "May Allah accept from us and from you"
كُلُّ عَامٍ وَأَنْتُمْ بِخَيْرٍ "May every year find you in good health"
جَعَلَنَا اللَّهُ وَإِيَّاكُمْ مِنَ الْعَائِدِينَ وَالْفَائِزِينَ "May Allah make us and you among those who return and succeed"

May the blessings of Allah fill your life with happiness, peace, and prosperity. Eid Mubarak!
